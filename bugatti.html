Bugatti

Bugatti is a French luxury automobile manufacturer known for producing high-performance sports cars. Founded in 1909 by Italian-born French automobile designer Ettore Bugatti, the brand is synonymous with engineering excellence and unparalleled craftsmanship.

Some of their most notable models include the Bugatti Veyron and Bugatti Chiron, both of which are celebrated for their speed and luxury. Bugatti continues to push the boundaries of automotive performance and design.

Notable Models
Bugatti Veyron

The Bugatti Veyron is renowned for its extreme speed and luxury, boasting a top speed of over 250 mph. It was one of the fastest production cars in the world when it was released.

Bugatti Chiron

The Bugatti Chiron is the successor to the Veyron and is known for its incredible performance, featuring a quad-turbocharged W16 engine that produces 1,479 horsepower.

Bugatti Divo

The Bugatti Divo is a more agile and dynamic version of the Chiron, focusing on handling and cornering while still delivering extraordinary performance.

Achievements

Bugatti has set numerous speed records and has been recognized for its innovation in automotive engineering. The Veyron Super Sport held the title of the world's fastest production car, and the Chiron continues to push the limits of performance.
